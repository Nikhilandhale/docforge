# Roadmap

DocForge is currently in MVP stage. The next milestones focus on quality, scalability, and broader format support.

## Short term

- Improve parsing robustness for edge cases and larger PDFs
- Refine chunking and retrieval quality for better grounding
- Expand test coverage around parsing and retrieval behavior
- Add clearer error handling and operational guidance

## Medium term

- Add support for additional document formats beyond PDF
- Improve answer grounding and retrieval relevance
- Introduce asynchronous processing for larger workloads
- Strengthen observability, deployment guidance, and production readiness
